> [!IMPORTANT] Mission Statement
> ProjectOmu is a non-profit, donation-funded gaming community focused on creating a welcoming and positive place for people to play and connect. We pride ourselves especially on inclusivity and LGBTQ+ inclusiveness. We host and maintain SS14 servers (OmuStation), and a variety of game servers whilst fostering a community driven environment where our members can socialize, and enjoy playing games together. We support the games we all love by engaging with the community's players, and contributors by focusing all excess donations on bounties for the community.
